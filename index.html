About

H! I'm KiranKumar Gonti

“I really believe there are things nobody would see if I didn’t photograph them.”

When people ask what kind of photographer I am, I usually answer with, "Well I shoot a little bit of everything", you name it.... engagement & weddings, events, portrait, candid.... the list goes on!
